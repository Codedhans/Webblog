# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 23, 2026**

At **The Codedhans**, accessible from {{ site.url }}, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by The Codedhans and how we use it.

## Log Files
The Codedhans follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Like any other website, The Codedhans uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.
